How to Play

Objective

Find and fix the root cause of the system outage for each round. A new outage occurs each round. Score as many points as you can before the Business Impact meter fills up.

Your Role

Your chosen role gives you a different number of tool uses per round, specializing you in certain areas of observability.

Gameplay

  1. Observe Symptoms: At the start of a round, some services will show symptoms (yellow). The root cause is hidden.
  2. Use Tools: Click a tool (Metrics, Logs, Trace) from the right panel, then click on a service node in the graph to use it. This consumes one use for that tool.
  3. Deduce the Cause: Use the clues from your tools to figure out which service is the true source of the failure.
    • Metrics: Reveal raw numbers on a service. Good for finding which services are unhealthy.
    • Logs: Give specific error messages, often pointing to a connected service.
    • Traces: Show the exact path of a request, highlighting the point of failure.
  4. Remediate: Once you think you've found the root cause, select the node and click the "Remediate" button. If you're right, you score points and a new round begins. If you're wrong, you take a big hit to Business Impact!
